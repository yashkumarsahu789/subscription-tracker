About Subscription Tracker

Subscription Tracker is a free, privacy-focused app designed to help you manage all your subscriptions, bills, and recurring payments in one place. Our mission is to empower users to take control of their finances, avoid missed payments, and eliminate unwanted charges.

Our Story

Founded by a team passionate about financial wellness, Subscription Tracker was created after realizing how easy it is to lose track of digital subscriptions, streaming services, SaaS tools, and utility bills. We wanted to build a simple, secure, and user-friendly solution for everyone.

Why Choose Subscription Tracker?

  • Completely free to use, with no hidden fees
  • Multi-currency and multi-platform support
  • Automatic payment reminders and exportable data
  • Privacy-first approach—your data is never sold
  • Responsive support and regular updates

We’re committed to helping you manage your money smarter. For feedback, support, or partnership inquiries, contact us anytime!

Our Mission

We believe in financial transparency and control. Our mission is to help you:

  • Never miss a payment deadline
  • Identify unused or forgotten subscriptions
  • Save money by cancelling unnecessary services
  • Gain complete visibility of your recurring expenses

100% Free

Subscription Tracker is completely free to use. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no credit card required. We built this tool to help people, not to profit from them.
