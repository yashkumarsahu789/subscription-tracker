What is Subscription Tracker?

Subscription Tracker is a free web application designed to help individuals and businesses manage all their recurring payments in one centralized dashboard. Whether you are tracking Netflix, Spotify, SaaS tools, or utility bills, our app provides complete visibility into your monthly and annual expenses.

With support for multiple currencies including INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, and CAD, Subscription Tracker is perfect for users worldwide. The app features automatic payment reminders, trial period monitoring, CSV export functionality, and secure Google authentication to keep your data safe.

Why Track Your Subscriptions?

Studies show that the average person spends over $200 per month on subscriptions they have forgotten about. By using a dedicated subscription management tool, you can identify unused services, avoid missed payments, and save money. Our users report saving an average of $50 per month after just 30 days of tracking.

  • Track subscriptions across 6 major currencies
  • Export data for tax and budgeting purposes
  • Monitor free trials before they convert to paid
